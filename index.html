How does it work?

The service uses webRTC, making possible to establish a peer to peer connection between the users, the only server used in the operation is and websocket that only connects the two peers, none of the data passes between this server, all the connection goes encripted between each peer, assecuring the privacy of the data.

None of the ips nor the registers of the users is kept by the server, once you disconnect from the site, the information about your peer is deleted
